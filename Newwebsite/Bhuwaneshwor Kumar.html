Bhuwaneshwor Kumar

Bhuvneshwar Kumar Singh was born on 5 February 1990.He is an Indian cricketer who plays all formats of the game.He plays for Uttar Pradesh in domestic cricket for Sunrises Hyderabad in the IPL.He is a right-arm medium bowler who swings the ball both ways efficiently, with his inswingers more effective than outswing, especially adept at creating late swing and a useful lower order right-handed batsman.

His birthplace is at Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, India. His full name is Bhuwaneshwor Kumar Singh Gurjar. His nickname is Bhuvi. His batting style is right handed and balling style is right-arm fast-medium. His role is balling and all rounder. His T20I shirt no. is 15. He has taken 132 wickets in ODI matches.
