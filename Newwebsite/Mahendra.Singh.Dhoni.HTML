Mahendra Singh Dhoni(MSD)

He was born on 7 July 1981 on Ranchi,India.He is now aged 38.His wife is Sakshi Dhoni.He was Married in 2010.His height is 5 feet 10 inches.(1.78m)His ODI shirt no. is 7.His nickname are Mahi,Captain Cool,Thala.His role is Wicket-keeper and batsman.His batting style is right-handed.

He is commonly known as MS Dhoni,he is an Indian cricketer who captained the Indian national team in limited overs formats from 2007 to 2016 and in Test Cricket from 2008 to 2014.He was named as the captain of the ICC World Test XI in 2009,2010 and 2013.Dhoni is one of the highest run scorers in One Day Internationals(ODI) with more than 10,000 runs scored and is considered an effective"finisher" in limited over formats.He is also regarded by some as one of the best Wicket-keepers
